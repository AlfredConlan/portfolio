A little bit about me...

I am an experienced Software Developer with the ability to pick up new languages and technologies as needed. Hoping to join an innovative company remotely, I am currently upskilling in Full-Stack Web Development.

I started writing code with BASIC in 1985 on a Commodore 128 I bought while in the Army. I instantly fell in love! Later on, sitting around the fire station, The IBM PS2 became my accomplice. In 1995, I made it official when I got my first programming job using Access and VBA. I spent the next 15 years as a contractor for various companies mostly creating desktop software using Visual Basic and SQL Server. In 2010, my dad became very ill and I returned home to help him. There were no programming jobs in my hometown, so I used the skills I had learned on the fire department years earlier to start my own building inspection business. However, I kept longing to return to my love of programming. With the growth of remote work due to the pandemic, I knew my chance had arrived. I am currently attending DigitalCrafts' Full Stack Web Development bootcamp to upgrade my skills to modern technologies. I'm also working as an intern for the Federal Emergency Management Agency to get some modern, real-world experience. I'm not as young as I once was, but I'm pretty sure I still have a few good lines of code left in me! 😁

In my spare time, I enjoy riding mountain bikes as well as hiking and camping with my 2 wonderful dogs, Pippin and Sam.
